Collection Name: finalized collections/MA Petersham, Nym Cooke collection INVENTORY (1)

------------------------------------Collection Description------------------------------------
Pre-1821 American Sacred Music 
in the collection of Nym Cooke
January 2019

incipits for sacred tunes are tenor voice, unless otherwise specified or tenor is not present
TCTB = treble, counter, tenor, bass (vocal parts always listed from top down)
5-6 = slurred notes (setting one syllable)
5_|5 = single note tied across a bar line
“,” in a string of scale degree numbers = end of one line of text, start of the next
4tr = trill on the 4th degree of the scale
5+U1 = 2 notes at once in a part
n4 or n7 = natural sign before the 4th or 7th degree of the scale, raising it a half-step
(6) = grace note
 = noteworthy
ASMI = Britton, Lowens, and Crawford, American Sacred Music Imprints, 1698-1810: A Bibliography (American Antiquarian Society, 1990)
